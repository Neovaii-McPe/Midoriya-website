Midoriya`s commands

Here is Midoriya`s command list.

Actually, there are not many commands yet, but we are looking forward to increase the amount of them.

– Mi!kick *nick* *reason* - it kicks a person from your server, with a reason

– Mi!ban *nick* *reason* - it bans a person from your server, with a reason

– Mi!unban *nick* - it unbans a person, which was banned at your server.

– Mi!youtube - it does a cool thing, you can check it out with yourself :)
